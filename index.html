🙂About

Based on multi-modal, high spatio-temporal resolution remote sensing images, my research interests mainly include:

  • Intelligent processing of remote sensing data: Low-quality image restoration; Multimodal image registration.
  • Real-time remote sensing processing in orbit: Semantic segmentation; Target/anomaly identification; Change detection.
  • Remote sensing monitoring of the environment: Inversion of terrestrial ecological parameters; Large-scale ecological impact analysis.
